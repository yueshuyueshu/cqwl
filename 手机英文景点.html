Chongqing attractions

Hongya Cave is located in Yuzhong District, Chongqing City, built by the mountain, like a group of stilted buildings, with bright lights at night, just like a realistic version of Spirited Away.

Hongya Cave

Located in Yuzhong District, Chongqing Liberation Monument is a monument to the victory of the Anti-Japanese War, symbolizing the history and culture of Chongqing, and is a prosperous commercial center and landmark.

Liberation Monument

Chongqing Ciqikou Ancient Town, located in Shapingba District, is a famous cultural town with a history of 1,000 years, famous for its Ming and Qing Dynasty architecture, traditional snacks and folk culture, and is a must-visit place for Chongqing tourism.
