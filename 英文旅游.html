Chongqing tourist route

Get on the train from Chongqing North Railway Station South Square Station, take the Inner Ring of the Rail Transit Ring Line (Chongqing Library direction), go to Wulidian Station after 2 stops, transfer to Line 6 (Chayuan direction), go to Xiaoshizi Station after 3 stops, and walk or take a bus to Jiefangbei.

Get on the train from Chongqing North Railway Station South Square Station, take the inner ring of the rail transit loop (Chongqing Library direction), go to Wulidian Station after 2 stops, transfer to Line 6 (Chayuan direction), go to Ciqikou Station after 3 stops, and walk to Ciqikou Ancient Town.
